# THREAT MODEL TEMPLATE

## 1 Asset Definition

List what needs protection

1 Personal identity information
2 Accounts and credentials
3 Devices and systems
4 Communications and activity logs

---

## 2 Adversary Definition

Identify potential observers

1 Casual attackers
2 Targeted individuals or groups
3 Automated scraping systems
4 Advanced surveillance entities

---

## 3 Attack Surface

Define how exposure can happen

1 Network exposure
2 Behavioral patterns
3 Metadata leakage
4 Human error

---

## 4 Risk Evaluation

For each asset determine

1 Likelihood of exposure
2 Impact if exposed
3 Ease of exploitation

---

## 5 Defense Strategy

Define protection methods

1 Separation of identities
2 Minimization of shared data
3 Secure communication channels
4 Controlled operational behavior

---

## 6 Output

A clear mapping between assets threats vulnerabilities and protections

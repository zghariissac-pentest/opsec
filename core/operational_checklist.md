# OPERATIONAL CHECKLIST

## 1 Pre Operation

1 Confirm identity separation
2 Confirm clean environment
3 Verify no residual data exposure
4 Ensure no active personal sessions

---

## 2 Active Operation

1 Use isolated environment only
2 Avoid cross referencing identities
3 Keep communication minimal and structured
4 Avoid behavioral repetition patterns

---

## 3 Data Handling

1 Store only required information
2 Remove metadata from all files
3 Encrypt sensitive outputs immediately
4 Avoid storing unnecessary logs

---

## 4 Network Safety

1 Verify connection route before use
2 Ensure no accidental leaks of real location data
3 Avoid mixing operational traffic with personal traffic

---

## 5 Post Operation

1 Clear temporary data
2 Remove cached artifacts
3 Review exposure risks
4 Log only safe summaries if needed

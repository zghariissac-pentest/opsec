# KILL CHAIN MAPPING

## 1 Reconnaissance

Information gathering phase where external data is collected about a target or system

---

## 2 Resource Preparation

Preparation of infrastructure or environment required for interaction or testing

---

## 3 Delivery Phase

Transmission of interaction into the target environment through communication or data flow

---

## 4 Exploitation Phase

Triggering of weaknesses in systems behavior or configuration

---

## 5 Establishment Phase

Creation of persistence or continuous access inside the environment

---

## 6 Communication Phase

External coordination or data exchange between system and external point

---

## 7 Objective Phase

Final goal execution which may include data extraction system control or analysis completion
